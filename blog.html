Why go to Greece on vacation?

May 23th Łukasz

If you are wondering where to go on vacation in 2022 then you don't have to anymore. Here are some of the reasons why you should choose Greece for your holiday vacation.

Greece enjoys good weather most of the year. The sun shines more than 250 days a year and the Greek islands are considered to be the sunniest region in Europe.

1. Beautiful beaches in Greece

Greece is famous for its stunning beaches and crystal clear waters in all shades of blue. Whether you love soft sand, pebbles or stones, you are sure to find the perfect holiday in Greece for you. You will find as many as 380 throughout Greece. Among the most famous Greek beaches are: Wreck Bay on Zakynthos, Myrtos Beach on Kefalonia, the Red Beach on Santorini and the pink beach of Elafonisi on Crete.

2. Greece's glorious history and impressive monuments

A vacation in Greece is an excellent choice for history lovers. At every turn you will find magnificent monuments from the time of the Greeks' greatest glory. The most famous Greek building is the Acropolis in Athens - the birthplace of our modern civilization. In each region you will find plenty of amazing places to discover.

3. Easy to get to Greece

Thanks to very good air connections we can move from rainy Poland to sunny Greece in just a few hours. There are daily flights from Athens to the most popular islands and to the biggest cities in mainland Greece. Many Greek islands can also be reached by ferry from the port of Piraeus. Thanks to this you can easily and in a relatively short time enjoy the Greek sun while sipping a drink on one of the beaches. Greece for vacations is just perfect!

4. Cheap vacations in Greece

Vacations in Greece are cheap compared to other European countries. Whether you prefer an All Inclusive vacation or rather travel on your own, you will find hotels and guesthouses for every budget. Booking holidays in Greece well in advance you can save up to 30-40% discount. You can also find many Last Minute Greece tours on travel agencies which will guarantee you a cheap vacation in Greece.

In Greece, everyone will find a way to have their dream vacation!

Car rental in Greece

April 12th Łukasz

Renting a car in Greece will give you a sense of freedom while traveling and allow you to stop wherever you feel like.

Greece is a wonderful holiday destination. Crystal clear sea, wonderful beaches, sunshine and delicious cuisine guarantee a successful holiday. If you like to discover new places and travel on your own terms off the popular tourist routes, a great solution for you will be renting a car.

When is it worth renting a car and what should I pay attention to when picking up a car from a rental agency?

It's worth renting a car if you're planning a round trip where you'll visit several places in Greece. But you should also pay attention to the following notes:

  • Greeks are known for their temper behind the wheel. Especially large Greek cities are heavily congested.
  • There are heavy fines for disregarding traffic laws.
  • Moving by car through the Greek cities requires from a driver not only a lot of experience but also the ability to stay calm.

Renting a car in Greece - what should you know?

  1. The minimum age for a driver to rent a car in Greece is 21 years old.
  2. Regardless of age, the driver must have held a license for a minimum of 12 months.
  3. When planning a car rental in Greece, make sure that you have enough money on your credit card to cover the cost of the car rental and the deposit.

The kilometer limit - if you are planning a trip around the Greek island, you will probably not use the kilometer limit. However, if you plan to drive a longer distance, make sure that your rental company will not charge you for the additional kilometers.
